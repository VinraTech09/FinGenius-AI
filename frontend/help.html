Help Center

Getting Started

1. Create an account: Sign up using your email and password.

2. Add transactions: Start by adding your income and expenses.

3. View dashboard: See your financial health score and insights.

4. Use AI chatbot: Ask questions about your finances.

How to Add Income

1. Go to Dashboard

2. Click "Add Income" button

3. Select category (Salary, Freelancing, Investment, etc.)

4. Enter amount and description

5. Click "Add Income"

How to Add Expenses

1. Go to Dashboard

2. Click "Add Expense" button

3. Select category (Bills, Food, Transport, etc.)

4. Enter amount and description

5. Click "Add Expense"

Understanding Health Score

80-100 (Excellent): Your finances are in great shape!

60-79 (Stable): Good, but there's room for improvement.

40-59 (Risk): Needs attention. Review your spending.

Below 40 (Critical): Take immediate action!
