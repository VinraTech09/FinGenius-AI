Frequently Asked Questions

🚀 Getting Started

What is FinGenius AI?

FinGenius AI is an AI-powered financial management platform that helps you track expenses, analyze spending patterns, and get personalized financial insights using advanced machine learning algorithms. It provides features like health scoring, fraud detection, and smart predictions.

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button on the login page. Fill in your name, email address, and create a password. That's it! You can start using FinGenius AI immediately after registration.

Is FinGenius AI free to use?

Yes! We offer a free plan with basic features. You can upgrade to Pro (₹299/month) or Premium (₹599/month) for advanced AI insights, fraud detection, and detailed predictions.

How do I add my first transaction?

On your dashboard, click the "+ Add Income" or "+ Add Expense" buttons. Select the category, enter the amount, add a description (optional), and set the date. Your transaction will be instantly added!

🔐 Account & Security

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) and secure server infrastructure. Your financial data is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions to create a new password.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account at any time. Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. Note that this action is irreversible and all your data will be permanently removed.

How does two-factor authentication work?

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security. When enabled, you'll need to enter a verification code sent to your email or phone number along with your password when logging in.

🤖 AI Features

How does the AI Health Score work?

Our AI analyzes your income, expenses, savings ratio, and spending patterns to calculate a health score from 0-100. Scores above 70 are considered excellent, 50-70 are stable, 25-50 need attention, and below 25 are critical.

How accurate are the predictions?

The predictions improve as you add more transactions. With at least 3 months of data, the predictions become more reliable (typically 70-85% accurate). The accuracy percentage is shown on your dashboard.

How does fraud detection work?

Our AI monitors your spending patterns and flags any unusual transactions. If an expense doesn't match your typical behavior (like an unusually large amount or transactions in different locations), you'll receive an instant alert.

What can I ask the AI Chatbot?

You can ask the chatbot about: purchase suggestions ("Can I buy a 50000 phone?"), savings advice, expense analysis, income overview, budget recommendations, and general financial tips. The AI considers your financial situation for personalized advice.

How does the AI Companion help me?

The AI Companion is a floating assistant that appears on all pages. It provides contextual tips based on what you're doing - like suggesting savings tips when viewing expenses or offering quick actions for common tasks.

🔧 Troubleshooting

Why is my health score not updating?

The health score updates after each new transaction. If you've added transactions but the score hasn't changed, try refreshing the page. Make sure you have at least one income and one expense recorded.

Why aren't the predictions showing?

Predictions require at least 2-3 months of transaction data. Keep adding your income and expenses regularly. The more data you provide, the more accurate the predictions become.

The app is running slowly. What should I do?

Try these steps: 1) Clear your browser cache and cookies, 2) Use a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge), 3) Check your internet connection, 4) Disable browser extensions temporarily.

How do I contact support?

You can reach us through: 1) The Contact page on our website, 2) Email: support@fingenius.ai, 3) Live chat (available 9 AM - 9 PM IST), 4) Premium users get priority 24/7 support.
